To address the forecasted extreme heat index, Campus Director Dr. Eric Malo-oy ordered a shift to Alternative Delivery Mode tomorrow, May 28, under CD Memo 2025-076. In a sweeping structural reform, Technological University of the Philippines Visayas - University Student Government (TUPV-USG) to implement major changes beginning this year’s Higher Executive and Legislative Elections (HELE) and Lower Executive and Legislative Elections (LELE), as confirmed by USG President Julie Ann Jolo in an interview with the publication, May 20. HELE introduced three new top-level positions: Vice President for External Affairs, Vice President for Internal Affairs, and Senator for Student Rights and Welfare (StRaW). These changes were spearheaded by former USG President Niel Escala in consultation with other TUP campuses to identify the most effective positions to institutionalize. Illuminated by brilliant smiles and resounding with echoes of laughters. TUPV Security Guards, Janitors, and Staffs (PPGS) celebrate GABI NG MANGGAGAWA: PARANGAL AT PASASALAMAT—a Labor Day Celebration, held at the TUPV Training Center, May 9, 2025. The event was kickstarted with a fun 5-round Bingo match. Everyone was on the edge of their chairs, hoping to hear the winning number that would complete their round pattern. After being filled with joy, it was time to be filled with food. A hearty dinner was shared by everyone while vibing with a karaoke. In between the meals, the minor raffle draw took place, winners received lunchboxes and umbrellas. Continuing with the event, the Bingo match concluded with the blackout round that was followed after by the major raffle draw. Winners of the major prizes took home brand new home appliances—electric kettle, flat iron, and rice cooker. The event came to an end with everyone leaving—hearts full, stomachs full, and hands full. We hope that through this, we were able to show our appreciation and gratitude towards these amazing individuals. Mabuhay ang mga mangagawang TUPVians!

𝗔𝗗𝗩𝗜𝗦𝗢𝗥𝗬 | Regular holiday on June 6

In observance of "Eid'l Adha" or the "Feast of Sacrifice," President Ferdinand Marcos Jr. declared June 6, Friday, as a regular holiday via Proclamation No. 911, May 21. This is to allow the Filipino Islam community to celebrate one of its two greatest feasts following the date recommended by the National Commission of Muslim Filipinos. - Via | Jon Vincent Mamar The Philippine Artisan Visayas

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) about TUP Visayas

TUP Visayas is the Technological University of the Philippines - Visayas Campus, a state university specializing in engineering and technology in the Visayas region.
The campus is located in Captain Sabi Street, Zone 12, Talisay City, 6115 Negros Occidental, Philippines
TUP Visayas offers programs in engineering, engineering technology, automation and control.
Applicants need to submit required documents, take the university entrance exam, and follow the admission procedures announced on Facebook page.
Yes, TUP Visayas provides academic and financial assistance scholarships. Visit the scholarship office for details and application procedures.
