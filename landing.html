Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) about TUP Visayas

TUP Visayas is the Technological University of the Philippines - Visayas Campus, a state university specializing in engineering and technology in the Visayas region.
The campus is located in Captain Sabi Street, Zone 12, Talisay City, 6115 Negros Occidental, Philippines
TUP Visayas offers programs in engineering, engineering technology, automation and control.
Applicants need to submit required documents, take the university entrance exam, and follow the admission procedures announced on Facebook page.
Yes, TUP Visayas provides academic and financial assistance scholarships. Visit the scholarship office for details and application procedures.
