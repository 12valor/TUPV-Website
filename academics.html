ACADEMIC PROGRAMS AT TUP VISAYAS

The Technological University of the Philippines – Visayas (TUP Visayas) remains steadfast in its commitment to providing quality technological and professional education in the region. As a vital campus of the TUP System, it continues to develop academic programs that are responsive to the evolving demands of local and global industries.

TUP Visayas offers a range of undergraduate and diploma programs that emphasize practical skills, innovation, and industry-based training. The campus regularly reviews and updates its curriculum to ensure alignment with current technological trends and workforce requirements, particularly in the Visayas and nearby regions.

Through strong partnerships with industries, government agencies, and academic institutions, TUP Visayas ensures that its graduates are equipped not only with technical proficiency but also with the values of excellence, integrity, and social responsibility.

Academic Programs by College

COE (College of Engineering)

Course Course Description
BS in Electronics Engineering Designed to train graduates in the fundamentals of Electronics Engineering, with deep scientific understanding and professional responsibility.
BS in Mechanical Engineering Provides skills in design, power systems, combustion, heat transfer, manufacturing, project research, and environmental studies.
BS in Electrical Engineering Develops skills in electrical power generation, transmission, and wiring systems for various establishments.
BS in Computer Engineering Applies engineering principles in the design and integration of computer hardware and software systems.

COET (College of Engineering Technology)

Course Course Description
BS in Chemistry Prepares graduates for practice in the chemical industry and supports SME development through strong laboratory-based training.
BET major in Automotive Engineering Technology Focuses on automotive technologies and management with emphasis on mathematics, research, and modern industrial techniques.
BET major in Chemical Engineering Technology Supports industrial chemical operations through practical training and strong laboratory components.
BET major in Electrical Engineering Technology Applies Electrical Engineering principles in designing and maintaining electrical systems and installations.
BET major in Electronics Engineering Technology Covers principles of electronics, circuit analysis, communication systems, and licensure-oriented training.
BET major in Manufacturing Engineering Technology Supports manufacturing industries through a curriculum focused on automation and metal-oriented processes.
BET major in HVACR Engineering Technology Specializes in HVACR systems, focusing on design, testing, maintenance, and service for comfort and safety in indoor environments.
BET in Electromechanical Engineering Technology Equips graduates with skills in developing and operating automated electromechanical systems across industries.
BET major in Computer Engineering Technology Focuses on the integration of software and hardware in modern computing and control systems.

COAC (College of Automation and Control)

Course Course Description
BS in Mechatronics Engineering Trains students to design and integrate intelligent systems in robotics, automation, and smart devices.
BS in Instrumentation and Control Engineering Prepares graduates to develop and manage control systems used in industrial and process automation.
BET major in Mechatronics Engineering Technology Focuses on technological applications in modern computing, automation, and integration of mechatronic systems.
