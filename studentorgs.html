TUP-V Student Organizations

TUP Visayas is home to a diverse range of accredited student organizations that promote leadership, collaboration, and community involvement. These organizations reflect the values of excellence, service, and innovation, helping students grow both academically and personally.

Whether you're interested in academics, culture, sports, or advocacy, there's a student group at TUP-V waiting to welcome you. These organizations play a vital role in campus life, organizing events, workshops, and outreach programs throughout the academic year.

Through active participation, TUPians gain valuable experiences that prepare them to become well-rounded professionals and responsible citizens of society.
