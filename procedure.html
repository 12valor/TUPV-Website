The Office of Admissions assists students in gaining a realistic understanding of their abilities, aptitudes, interests, personality traits, and academic achievements.

The objectives are as follows: (1) to help students realize their full educational potential; (2) to identify students' strengths and weaknesses; and (3) to understand the implications of these traits to better support counselors in forming a comprehensive view of each student.
